Grid Layout

-This is the layout for the site, the screen is broken up horizontally into 6 columns, we chose 6 columns becasue it would be a good way to break up things into halves, thirds, and a smaller portion. There is no fixed width, it is scaled to fit the screen or the container it is in. When the screen reaches below 800 pixels, the collumns will adjust to fit the width of the screen.

Typography

Heading 1

-This heading is for primary sections. It is the Quicksand Family with the font size of 40, weight of 700/bold, and the color of #4d201d.

Heading 2

-This heading is for primary sections that require emphasis. It is the Quicksannd Family with the font size of 40, weight of 700/bold, and the color of #b2868d.

Heading 3

-This heading is for secondary groupings. It is the Quicksand Family with the font size of 30, weight of 500/normal, and the color of #4d201d.

Heading 4

-This heading is for secondary groupings that require emphasis. It is the Quicksand Family with the font size of 30, weight of 500/normal, and the accent color of #b2868d.

Heading 5

-This heading is for less important sub-groupings, or li elements It is the Quicksand Family with the font size of 20, weight of 400/Italic, and the color of #4d201d.

Heading 6

-This heading is for less important sub-groupings or li elements of different importance than h5. It is the Quicksand Family with the font size of 20, weight of 400/Italic, with the accent color of #b2868d.
